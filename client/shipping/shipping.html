Shipping & Returns

Shipping

At Swarn Jewelry, we offer free shipping across India on all orders. Your order will be processed and shipped within 2-3 business days.

We use reputable carriers like FedEx, DTDC, and India Post to ensure your purchase arrives safely and on time.

If you have any questions or concerns about your order's shipping status, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team.

Returns

We want you to be completely satisfied with your Swarn Jewelry purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your item, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer support team at support@swarn.com to obtain a return authorization number.
  2. Package the item securely in its original condition and packaging.
  3. Ship the item back to us at the address provided by our customer support team.
  4. Once we receive and inspect the item, we will process your refund. Please allow 7-10 business days for the refund to be credited to your original payment method.

Please note that custom-made or personalized items are not eligible for returns. Additionally, all jewelry must be returned in its original condition to qualify for a refund.

If you have any questions or concerns about our returns policy, feel free to reach out to our customer support team.
